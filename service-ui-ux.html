FAQ

Common Questions

What design tools do you use?
+
Figma is our primary tool for all UI/UX work. We also use FigJam, Maze for testing, and Lottie for animations.
Do you provide design systems?
+
Yes. Every UI project includes a scalable design system with components, tokens, and documentation.
Can you redesign an existing product?
+
Absolutely. We perform UX audits, identify pain points, and redesign with clear rationale behind every decision.
Will the designs be developer-ready?
+
Yes. Handoff includes annotated Figma files, exported assets, spacing specs, and interactive prototype links.
